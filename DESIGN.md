# CoproSync Design Language

---

## Overview

Architectural Minimalism meets Operational Intelligence. The UI evokes a high-end SaaS dashboard—a mix of **Mission Control** precision and **Premium Hospitality** aesthetics.

## Colors

The palette is rooted in deep structural neutrals with vibrant functional accents.

- **Primary (#4F46E5):** Used for primary actions and brand presence.
- **Secondary (#0F172A):** The "Deep Space" color for headers and core structural elements.
- **Neutral (#F8FAFC):** The "Limestone" foundation, providing a clean, professional canvas.

## Typography

We use **Geist Sans** for its technical precision and modern feel.

- **Headings:** Always heavy (Black/900) and tight (tracking-tighter) to evoke authority.
- **Labels:** Uppercase with wide tracking for a "technical readout" aesthetic.

## Responsive Strategy

- **Mobile:** Single column layouts, oversized touch targets (48px+), and bottom-sheet drawers for complex actions.
- **Desktop:** Multi-column grids, hover states for interaction cues, and sidebars for navigation.
